Japan's Surrender

1945



(Shigemitsu)

On September 2nd, 1945, less than a month after the nuclear bomb was dropped on Hiroshima and Nagasaki, The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Japan officially signed a declaration of surrender to the United States. The consequences of the Bombs had been so great that they completely counteracted the long term effects of Imperialism, Nationalism, and Militarism in Japan.
