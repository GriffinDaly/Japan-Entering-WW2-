Post-Tokugawa Japan

1853


This distorted world atlas is a map by japanese cartographer Abe Yasuyuki immediately before the replacement of the Tokugawa government in Japan. It shows how isolated Japan was at this point in its history. All information of the outside world was trickled in from the few Portuguese Traders the country recieved.
