Thesis: The conjectures, conjunctions, and accidents in Japan’s history tracing as far back as it can go all directly impact the patterns and cultural themes that developed within the country, namely ethnocentrism, nationalism, and colonization, and specifically these patterns and themes were what pushed Japan to join the second world war.
